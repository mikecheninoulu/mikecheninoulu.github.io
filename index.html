Haoyu Chen | University of Oulu
chen.haoyu@oulu.fi

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at CMVS, University of Oulu. I received my PhD from University of Oulu , Finland, where I was advised by Academy Professor Guoying Zhao. During my PhD study, I visited CEL, TUDelft, the Netherlands. Prior to that, I received the B.E. degree from China University of Geosciences, China, and Master degree from University of Oulu, Finland.

My research interests include Machine Learning, Human Behaviour Analysis, Emotion AI and Adversarial Learning.
